About Us

We’re the professionals the professionals ask for…

Founded by Trust Kakaza, Hambanathi Shuttle Services was built on three core values: Safety. Punctuality. Friendliness. Over and above our core values, we believe that every journey should begin with peace of mind.

So, whether you’re catching an early flight from OR Tambo International, heading to Lanseria International Airport, dropping your children safely at school, a tourist looking to see the popular and off-the-beaten path sights and sounds, or travelling to an executive meeting across town, we’ll get you there safely, comfortably, and on time.

Every member of our team is carefully handpicked, trained, and dedicated to providing exceptional service with the Hambanathi Shuttle Service’s personal touch. From executives to schoolchildren, we transport every passenger with care, respect, and responsibility.
